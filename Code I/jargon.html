Jargon

Below are some commonly used terms and what they mean.
  • Draft: When each team selects their players.
  • PPR: A type of leauge where players score points for catching the ball.
  • Projection: How many points a player is projected to score by fantasy football analysts.
  • Waive Wire: A place where players who aren't on a fantasy team in the leauge can be picked up by any team.
